Family of 59-year-old in Delhi donates organs, saves five lives

The family of a brain dead man from Delhi donated his organs at AIIMS, giving new life to at least five people. The 59-year-old man was in an accident with his son on April 30 and was declared brain dead a few hours later. His heart went to Indraprastha Apollo Hospital while his liver was allocated to the Army Hospital.

“The accident has been a huge shock for our family, but it was important that the spirit that my father lived by throughout his life be carried on his death too. He was very kind and giving, and he would have wanted to help as many people as he could,” said Nagendra Singh, his son.
